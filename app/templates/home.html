Control Your LED Lights with Ease

Scyra is a user-friendly web interface for WLED- controlled LEDs that puts privacy first.

Follow these steps to get started:

1. Enter your WLED address

Simply enter the IP address of your WLED-connected LED light strip.

2. Create your light design

Select colors and adjust brightness within a simple to understand visual UI.

3. Save or run your design

Copy your preset into WLED, or activate your preset with just 1 click!
