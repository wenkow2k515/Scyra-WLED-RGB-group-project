About Scyra

Our Mission

Scyra was created with a dual mission: to make LED lighting control accessible to everyone and to enhance human wellbeing through the science of color psychology. We believe that smart lighting should not only be simple and intuitive but also contribute positively to your mood and mental state.

Our web interface requires no downloads or specialized knowledge - just connect to your WLED-compatible device and start creating beautiful, mood-enhancing lighting scenes in seconds. Whether you're looking to boost productivity, create a relaxing atmosphere, or set the perfect ambiance for any occasion, Scyra puts the power of light and color at your fingertips.

Key Features

No Account Required

Use Scyra without creating an account. Your settings stay in your browser by default.

RGB Color Control

Full-spectrum color adjustment with intuitive controls for your LED lights.

LED Segment Management

Control individual LED segments or groups with precise settings.

Effects Library

Multiple pre-designed light effects and animations for any occasion.

Mood Analysis

Intelligent system that analyzes and suggests optimal lighting based on your desired mood and activity.

Privacy First

Unlike other smart lighting solutions, Scyra is designed with your privacy in mind:

  • No mandatory account creation
  • Minimal data collection
  • Local storage of settings by default
  • Optional cloud features only if you want them
  • No tracking or analytics

We believe that controlling your lights shouldn't mean sacrificing your privacy.

Open Source

Scyra is built upon the amazing WLED project, an open-source solution for controlling WS2812B and many other types of digital RGB LEDs.

We're committed to the open-source philosophy and are grateful to the WLED community for making projects like ours possible.

Color Psychology & Mood Analysis

We understand the profound impact colors have on human emotions and well-being. Scyra incorporates color psychology principles to help you create lighting environments that positively influence your mood.

Mood Enhancement

Choose colors scientifically proven to enhance focus, relaxation, creativity, or energy based on your needs.

Intelligent Suggestions

Our system suggests color combinations based on the time of day, season, and desired emotional response.

Whether you're looking to improve productivity in a home office, create a calming bedroom environment, or set the perfect mood for entertainment, Scyra's color psychology features help optimize your lighting for wellbeing.
